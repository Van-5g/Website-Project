Tacticals

Smoke Grenade

Creates a dense smoke screen upon detonation. This smoke screen obstructs the line of sight for both players and enemies within its area of effect. Perfect for rushing.

Stim Shot

Defends against incoming enemy projectiles. This includes lethal and tactical grenades, as well as launcher projectiles.

Stim Shot

Increases health regeneration and movement speed for a short period of time.

Lethals

Trip Mine

Magnetic mine that launches into the air and detonates when an enemy interrupts the beam.

Molotov Cocktail

When thrown, the Molotov Cocktail creates a fire zone on the ground, dealing damage over time to any players within its area of effect.

C4

C4 can be manually detonated by the player, causing a significant explosion in its vicinity. The explosion deals damage to all enemies within its blast radius.
