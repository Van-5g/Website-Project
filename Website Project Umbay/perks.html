Blue Perk

Dead Silence

Grants silent movement when walking, crouching or going prone. Sound spread distance is reduced when sprinting.

High Alert

Your vision pulses when enemies outside of your view see you. Mark enemies, their equipment, and their scorestreaks by aiming down your sights.

Persistence

Scorestreak progression doesn't reset upon death, but each Scorestreak costs double the regular amount and can only be used once per match. Scorestreaks will also be divided into 3 ranks and you can only equip one of each rank at the same time.

Red Perks

Fast Recover

Increases health recovery by 35%.

Flak Jacket

Explosive damage is reduced by 35%. throwing back a grenade resets its time and reduces damage from Molotov Cocktails.

Pin Point

Outlines enemies in red when dealing or taking weapon damage and enhances aim assist.
